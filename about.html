About BeMoreSmart

BeMoreSmart is a curated learning library built for long-term use.

It exists to solve a simple problem: finding good learning resources once — and not having to search for them again.

Instead of chasing algorithms, trends, or endless recommendations, BeMoreSmart focuses on collecting high-quality resources and organising them in a way that supports sustained, independent learning.

This site is intentionally quiet, static, and distraction-free.

What this site is

BeMoreSmart is a reference library, not a course. It’s a starting point rather than a complete curriculum, and a personal knowledge archive designed to be revisited over months or years.

Resources are grouped by topic and discipline so you can save progress, return later without losing context, and explore new areas without overwhelm.

You are meant to dip in, step away, and come back.

What this site is not

BeMoreSmart is not a replacement for formal education, not a promise that every resource is “the best,” and not a platform optimised for speed-running knowledge.

Learning takes time. This site respects that.

How resources are chosen

Resources are selected for clarity over hype, depth over virality, and usefulness for long-term learners.

Many links point to playlists, series, or creators that reward consistency rather than quick wins. Nothing on this site is autogenerated.

How to use this site effectively

  • Bookmark topics you’re actively studying
  • Save individual resources you want to return to
  • Avoid opening everything at once — this is a library, not a checklist
  • Let interests rotate naturally over time

Progress doesn’t have to be visible to be real.

A note on maintenance

BeMoreSmart is designed to be left alone.

It does not need constant updates to remain useful. New resources may be added occasionally, but stability matters more than freshness.
